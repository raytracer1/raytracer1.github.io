About Me

Hi, I'm Bijun Zheng — a content creator and SaaS reviewer based in China. I run this site to share honest, hands-on reviews of tools that help creators, bloggers, and small business owners grow online.

My Background

I've spent years testing and comparing software tools across email marketing, ecommerce, content creation, and AI automation. My work involves signing up for each tool, using it in real workflows, and documenting what works — and what doesn't — so readers can make informed decisions without wasting time or money.

Why I Built This Site

The SaaS landscape is crowded, and marketing copy often oversells features while glossing over limitations. I wanted a place where I could publish detailed, practical comparisons and tutorials that reflect actual usage rather than just vendor claims.

My focus areas include email marketing platforms (like Kit, formerly ConvertKit), AI-assisted content tools, and ecommerce tools for Shopify store owners. I publish comparison articles, step-by-step setup guides, and workflow demonstrations across written and video formats.
